How It Works

The Admission Process

We admit students throughout the year, subject to available places in each class. The process is designed to be clear, welcoming, and honest.

1

Contact Us or Visit the School

Call, email, or simply come in. We encourage a visit so you can see the school for yourself, walk through classrooms, and speak with our staff before making any decisions.

2

Collect and Submit the Application Form

Forms are available at the school office. Complete it and return it with the required supporting documents. Our office team will guide you through anything unclear.

3

Brief Assessment (P2 and above)

Students joining P2 or above sit a short, low-pressure assessment. This helps us understand their current level and place them appropriately — not to exclude them.

4

Receive Admission Confirmation

Once accepted, we provide a formal admission letter with all the details you need — fees, start date, uniform requirements, and any other essentials.

5

Complete Registration and Join

Pay the required fees to confirm your child's place. Then on the first day of term, your child is a Genesis student — and part of our school family.

What You Need

Admission Requirements

Joining Primary 1

  • Child must be at least 6 years old
  • Birth certificate or baptism card
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Immunisation card (recommended)
  • Parent or guardian identification
  • Completed admission form

Joining P2 to P7

  • Previous school report cards
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Birth certificate or baptism card
  • Two recent passport photographs
  • Parent or guardian identification
  • Completed admission form
School Fees

Fee Information

Detailed Fees Available on Request

Our fees are structured to reflect the quality of education we provide while remaining as accessible as possible. Fees vary by class level and cover tuition, learning materials, and the range of school activities. We do not have hidden charges.

For a full and current fee breakdown, please contact our office directly or visit the school. We are completely transparent about costs and happy to discuss what is included.

What Fees Cover

  • Quality instruction from trained and committed teachers
  • Core learning materials and textbooks for each level
  • Use of all school facilities including the computer lab
  • Sports, co-curricular, and cultural activities
  • Regular termly assessments and formal report cards
  • Parent-teacher consultations each term

Useful to Know

  • Fees are paid on a termly basis
  • Three terms per year following the Ugandan school calendar
  • Fee payment plans can be discussed with the school office
  • Uniform requirements and costs explained at admission
  • No surprise levies or undisclosed charges
  • All payments are formally receipted
Good to Know

Before You Enroll

Class Sizes

We maintain managed class sizes so each teacher can give genuine individual attention to every student.

School Calendar

Three terms per year following Uganda's national school calendar. Term dates shared at the start of each year.

School Uniform

All students wear the school uniform. Details, sourcing information, and costs are provided on admission.

Parent Communication

We run regular parent meetings, share termly reports, and maintain open channels for any concerns throughout the year.
