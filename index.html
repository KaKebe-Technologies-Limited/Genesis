Our School

A School Built Around Your Child

Genesis Primary School is a carefully run institution in Lira dedicated to providing quality education that prepares children for success — academically, morally, and personally. We believe every child deserves teachers who care, an environment that is safe, and an education that actually works.

Why Genesis?

What Sets Us Apart

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum follows national standards with modern methods that build critical thinking and genuine understanding — not just exam scores.

Qualified, Caring Teachers

Our educators are trained professionals who invest in each child individually. They don't just teach — they mentor, guide, and inspire.

Safe, Secure Campus

A properly managed, supervised environment where your child can focus entirely on learning and growing without worry.

Strong Moral Values

Integrity, discipline, and respect are taught alongside academics. We develop character — the kind of child who succeeds in life, not just class.

Thorough PLE Preparation

Upper primary students receive intensive, focused preparation for the Primary Leaving Examination with mock tests and guided revision.

Parent Communication

Regular progress reports, parent meetings, and open communication keep families fully informed and genuinely involved in their child's education.

Curriculum

Education That
Actually Prepares

We follow Uganda's national curriculum from P1 to P7, ensuring students are well-prepared for PLE and the next stage of their education.

  • English, Mathematics, and Science
  • Social Studies and Religious Education
  • Creative Arts and Physical Education
  • ICT and Computer Studies
  • Regular assessments and individual feedback
  • Structured PLE preparation for P6 & P7
